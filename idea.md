
# General Description

This is a little bundle of ideas for an alternative messaging app. In this app a longer, more slowpaced discussion would be encouraged. 

Letters will take predetermined time to deliver, for example 24 hours, this could also be a conversation specific setting that would allow users to decide their prefered delivery time. The opossing person will be notified instantly once a letter is on it's way to them. 

Letter could also be addressed to someone who isn't yet registered in this app. This would be done through an open letter board where entering the recepients name would present a collection of letter addressed to this person

To help with steady and continous conversations each persons contact list will have multiple possible orderings. One order would be reversed most recently conversed with. Another order would be where the person has received a letter but not yet send one back.
